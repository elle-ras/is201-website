Duke Basketball

My family has always loved Duke basketball, especially since both of my parent went there for the MBA programs. Because of that, I grew up watching Duke game, following players, and getting really into the sport. Over time, it made me love basketball even more, especially since I played myself and could relate to what I was watching.

Coach K & Why Duke is Special

One of the biggest reasons Duke basketball is so well known is because of Coach K. He coached at Duke from 1980 to 2022 and is considered one of the best coaches ever, winning five national championships.

What I think is cool is how consistent Duke has been over time. Even after Coach K, the program still stays competitive and relevant every year.

My Thoughts

I think Duke basketball is fun to watch because it always feels intense. The games are competitive and there is always something happening.
